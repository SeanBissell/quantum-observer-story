# THE QUANTUM OBSERVER
## An Interactive Story

### BACK COVER SUMMARY (NO SPOILERS)

---

**Steve is 72, retired, and living a comfortable life in Newcastle, Washington with his wife Nancy. His days are predictable, his routines familiar—until Tuesday morning, when reality begins to fracture.**

A book he donated decades ago reappears on his shelf. His one-eyed cat briefly has two eyes. Neighbors remember events that never happened. His blue Highlander is suddenly red. Small impossibilities multiply into undeniable proof: something is very wrong with reality itself.

**That night, Steve receives a cryptic note—written in his own handwriting—inviting him to meet himself at midnight.**

What follows is a journey through quantum timelines, philosophical questions about choice and regret, and an exploration of the lives Steve could have lived. With his wife Nancy by his side, Steve must navigate a reality where every decision he's ever made has created infinite branching possibilities—and all of them are suddenly converging.

**But why is this happening? And why now?**

As the boundaries between timelines dissolve, Steve will discover that some mysteries are deeply personal, some are profoundly universal, and the answer to "Did I make the right choices?" might be the wrong question entirely.

---

**A philosophical science fiction story about the roads not taken, the power of acceptance, and what it means to truly see your whole life from outside time.**

*Perfect for fans of Kurt Vonnegut, Philip K. Dick, and Ted Chiang.*

---

### AN INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCE

This isn't just a story to read—it's a story to experience. You can:

- **Ask questions** of characters and explore deeper
- **Make choices** about which paths to investigate
- **Interact with reality** as it shifts around you
- **Or simply observe** as the story unfolds naturally

The narrative adapts to your engagement, flowing whether you participate actively or prefer to simply read and absorb.
